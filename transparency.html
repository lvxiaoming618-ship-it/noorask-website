Transparency

NoorAsk pledges complete transparency in all fundraising and expenditure

Our Commitment

NoorAsk is a public-interest platform. We believe that trust is built on transparency. Every taka donated, every expense incurred, and every decision made is open to scrutiny by our donors, partners, and the community we serve.

This page is updated regularly to reflect the latest financial status of our mosque partnership fundraising. If you have any questions, please contact us.

How Funds Are Used

Every donation is allocated responsibly across four key areas

Product Development 40%

Building and maintaining the NoorAsk platform, including mobile apps, web infrastructure, and AI systems.

Content Production 30%

Creating verified Islamic educational content, translating materials, and producing multimedia resources.

Marketing 20%

Community outreach, representative support, mosque onboarding, and awareness campaigns.

Operations 10%

Administrative costs, legal compliance, accounting, and platform security.
