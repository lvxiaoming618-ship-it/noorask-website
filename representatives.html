Representatives

Be the local champion connecting mosques to NoorAsk in your community

Your Responsibilities

Four key ways you will help grow the NoorAsk network

Local Promotion

Spread awareness about NoorAsk within your city or district through community events, social media, and word of mouth.

Mosque Outreach

Identify and approach mosques that would benefit from partnering with NoorAsk. Present the platform and its value.

Signing Support

Assist mosques in completing the partnership application and guide them through the online agreement signing process.

Q&A Support

Be the first point of contact for mosque partners in your area, answering basic questions and escalating when needed.

Incentive Structure

Your dedication is recognized and rewarded

Milestone Reward Details
1st Mosque Signed Welcome Bonus One-time bonus upon successful onboarding of your first partner mosque.
5th Mosque Signed Achievement Bonus Additional bonus when you reach five active partner mosques in your region.
Monthly Contribution Stipend Monthly contribution based on active mosques under your care and engagement metrics.
