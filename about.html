About NoorAsk

A public-interest Islamic digital education platform serving the global Muslim community

Our Mission

NoorAsk is a public-interest Islamic digital education platform initiated by Jovi & Josh Waresoft Studio. We believe that every Muslim deserves easy access to reliable religious guidance, and that local mosques should be empowered to serve their communities through modern technology.

We are launching first in Bangladesh, using location-based services (LBS) to connect worshippers with their local mosques. By bridging the gap between traditional Islamic scholarship and contemporary digital tools, NoorAsk creates a trusted space for seeking knowledge.

Our platform is built on the principles of accessibility, authenticity, and community. We partner directly with mosques and qualified imams to ensure that every answer is grounded in sound Islamic knowledge and local understanding.

Core Features

Designed to strengthen the bond between mosques and their communities

  • LBS + Mosque Homepage: Find nearby mosques and explore their profiles, prayer times, events, and announcements.
  • Smart Q&A Matching (Unlimited): Ask any question and receive instant, AI-matched answers from verified Islamic sources — with no usage limits.
  • Imam Q&A: Submit questions directly to your local imam for personalized, authoritative guidance.
  • Anonymous or Named Questions: Choose whether to ask publicly, anonymously, or with your name — full flexibility and privacy.
  • Daily Verse Push: Receive a daily Quranic verse with reflection to keep your heart connected to the Book of Allah.
