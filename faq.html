Content

Technology

We are developing both iOS and Android apps. In the meantime, the platform is fully accessible through any mobile browser at noorask.online.

We use industry-standard encryption and never sell user data. Anonymous questions are not linked to any personal identifier. See our Privacy Policy for details.

We use Cloudflare and redundant hosting to ensure high availability. In the unlikely event of downtime, all data is safely backed up and will be restored.

Finance

NoorAsk is funded through voluntary donations from the community, grants from Islamic organizations, and strategic partnerships. We do not display advertisements.

Representatives

Any committed Muslim with good community standing, reliable internet access, and a passion for serving local mosques can apply. Prior sales or community experience is helpful but not required.

Approved representatives receive a digital onboarding kit, video training sessions, and access to a dedicated support channel. We also host monthly virtual meetups.

Initially, representatives focus on one city or district to ensure quality. High-performing representatives may be promoted to regional roles.
