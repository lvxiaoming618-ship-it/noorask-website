Why Partner With Us?

Tangible benefits for your mosque and community

Digital Showcase

Your mosque gets a dedicated homepage on NoorAsk — prayer times, events, announcements, and contact info, all in one place.

Online Da'wah Channel

Extend your mosque's voice beyond physical walls. Share sermons, articles, and religious reminders with a wider audience.

Congregation Engagement

Interact directly with your community through Q&A, polls, and feedback — strengthening relationships beyond Friday prayers.

Reward of Good Deeds

Every question answered, every guidance shared, every heart softened — all recorded as continuous charity (sadaqah jariyah).

Voluntary Fundraising

Accept transparent, voluntary donations from supporters to fund your mosque's activities and maintenance.
